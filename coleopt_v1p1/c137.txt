Fourteen species of this genus have been described; but many more doubtless exist. They are all minute insects of flattened form, living under the bark of trees; and they occur in widely different regions, tropical as well as subarctic, in both hemispheres.
1. Tachyta nana.
Bembidium nanum, Gyllenhal, Ins. Suecica, ii. p. 30; Dej. Sp. Gén. Col. v. p. 511.
