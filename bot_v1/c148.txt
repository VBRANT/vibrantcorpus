4. GALPHIMIA.
Galphimia, Cav. Ic. v. p. 61; Benth. et Hook. Gen. Plant. i. p. 254.
Shrubs and undershrubs, about a dozen species, chiefly found in Mexico and Central America, but extending to Brazil. The species appear to have been unduly multiplied by Bartling.
