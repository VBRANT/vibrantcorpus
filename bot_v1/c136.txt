Order XXVI. TILIACEÆ.
Tiliaceæ, Benth. et Hook. Gen. Plant. i. p. 228.
Trees or shrubs, rarely herbaceous. About 350 species, belonging to forty-five genera, distributed over all parts of the world except the frigid regions.
