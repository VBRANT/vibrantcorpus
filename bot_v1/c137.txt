2. TRIUMFETTA.
Triumfetta, Linn. Gen. Plant. n. 600; Benth. et Hook. Gen. Plant. i. p. 234.
Herbs, undershrubs, or shrubs. About forty or fifty species, dispersed in the tropics of both hemispheres, some having a wide range. Several of the following names will doubtless prove to be synonyms of others.
1. Triumfetta althæoides, Lam. Dict. iii. p. 420.
SOUTH MEXICO, Yucatan and Tabasco (Johnson, 58), without locality (Aschenborn).—Common in Tropical SOUTH AMERICA and the WEST INDIES. Hb. Kew.
2. Triumfetta botteriana, Turcz. in Bull. Soc. Nat. Mosc. 1859, i. p. 260.
SOUTH MEXICO, Orizaba (Botteri, 773). Hb. Kew.
